Specialized Dental Care

Initial Consultation & Diagnosis:

  1. Patient history review (medical & dental)

  2. Physical oral examination

  3. Diagnostic tests (X-rays, CT scans, or 3D imaging)

  4. Discussion of symptoms and concerns

Referral to a Specialist:

  • If needed, the general dentist refers the patient to a specialist such as:

    1. Endodontist (root canals)

    2. Periodontist (gum diseases)

    3. Orthodontist (braces & aligners)

    4. Oral Surgeon (tooth extraction, implants, or jaw surgery)

    5. Prosthodontist (dentures, crowns, bridges)

    6. Pediatric Dentist (children's dentistry)

Treatment Planning:

  1. Specialist explains treatment options

  2. Discussion of costs, risks, and benefits

  3. Custom treatment plan based on the patient's needs

Specialized Treatment:

  1. Procedure performed based on diagnosis

  2. Use of advanced technology (lasers, digital impressions, etc.)

  3. Local or general anesthesia, if required

  4. Follow-up care and monitoring

Post-Treatment Care & Maintenance:

  1. Instructions for aftercare (diet, oral hygiene, medication)

  2. Follow-up visits to monitor progress

  3. Preventive care recommendations (regular checkups, cleanings)
