Specialized Departments

Patient Registration & Initial Assessment

  1. Patient details recorded (personal & medical history)

  2. Initial consultation to determine the need for specialized care

  3. Referral from a general practitioner or dentist if required

Diagnosis & Specialist Consultatio

  1. Physical examination and diagnostic tests (X-rays, MRI, CT scans, blood tests, etc.)

  2. Specialist reviews findings and discusses symptoms with the patient

  3. Identification of the condition requiring specialized treatment

Treatment Planning

  1. Detailed explanation of treatment options and procedures

  2. Discussion of costs, risks, and expected outcomes

  3. Custom treatment plan based on the patient's condition and health status

Specialized Treatment & Procedures

  1. Treatment is carried out by specialists in their respective departments

  2. Use of advanced technology and techniques specific to the field

  3. Surgery, therapy, or medication depending on the condition

Post-Treatment Care & Rehabilitation

  1. Monitoring of recovery progress

  2. Follow-up appointments and necessary adjustments to the treatment

  3. Rehabilitation, physical therapy, or lifestyle recommendations if needed

Preventive Care & Long-Term Management

  1. Education on disease prevention and health maintenance

  2. Regular check-ups and screenings to prevent complications

  3. Lifestyle modifications and continued specialist support
